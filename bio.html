  • 1993. Born in Moscow

  • 2010-2015. Undegrad at the Moscow State University, Faculty of Mathematics and Mechanics.

  • 2015-2019. PhD student of Nikolay Vereshchagin, also at the Moscow State University. Working on complexity theory, mostly on communication complexity. Defended a thesis called "Comparison of Communication, Information and Decision Tree Complexity".

  • 2020-2021. Postdoc at the Department of Computer Science of University of Warwick, under the supervision of Marcin Jurdzinski. Working on games on graphs.

  • 2022. Postdoc at the Steklov Mathematical Institute, under the supervision of Vladimir Podolskii. Working on sorting networks and monotone circuits.

  • 2022-now. Postdoc at the Pontifical Catholic University of Chile, supervised by Pablo Barceló and Cristóbal Rojas.
